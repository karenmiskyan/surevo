Direction D

Vero — the agent

A friendly, stylised 2D agent character beside the wordmark. Gives Surevo a face customers trust and remember — and a mascot you can animate in chat, ads, and onboarding.
Trust · Memorable
Research-backed: handing your sales to a bot feels risky. A stylised 2D character is the evidence-based default for AI-agent avatars — it humanises the tech, builds trust, and “a face is remembered more easily than a traditional logo.” Blue reinforces reliability.

Direction E

The Sure Seal

A premium “guarantee seal” monogram — navy and gold, with the checkmark at its heart. Reads as verified, trusted, high-end. Positions Surevo as the concierge-grade choice, not a cheap plugin.
Premium · Trusted
Business logic: “Surevo” = sure = a guaranteed sale. A seal/badge form signals certainty and verification — exactly the reassurance a store owner needs. Navy + gold is the documented palette of luxury and prestige, separating you from playful, low-cost rivals.
