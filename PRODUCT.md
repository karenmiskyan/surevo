# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Israeli WooCommerce store owners doing roughly ₪100K-₪1M per month, and digital agencies that manage active WooCommerce stores. They arrive while deciding whether they can trust a managed AI sales agent for website and WhatsApp conversations.

## Product Purpose

Surevo is a done-for-you AI sales agent for WooCommerce and WhatsApp in Israel. It connects to the store catalog, inventory, policies, and commerce systems where available, answers in natural Hebrew, moves conversations toward orders, and hands off to a human when the information is uncertain. The website should convert qualified stores and agencies into free sales audit requests.

## Brand Personality

Local, careful, revenue-minded, and direct. Surevo should feel like a serious Israeli commerce partner with a friendly sales assistant named Vero, not like a generic AI chatbot demo.

## Anti-References

Not a self-serve plugin, not an English-first SaaS translated into Hebrew, not fake metric hype, not robot clipart, not vague AI promises, and not SEO pages that repeat thin content.

## Design Principles

- Trust before spectacle: show that Surevo uses real store information and hands off instead of inventing.
- One conversion path: every page should naturally lead to the free sales audit or a qualified partner conversation.
- Hebrew-first and Israel-specific: WooCommerce, WhatsApp, shekels, Israeli integrations, and local buying behavior.
- Managed service, not shelfware: emphasize setup, testing, optimization, reporting, and accountability.
- Vero should reassure: visible, friendly, and useful, but never childish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA, native RTL Hebrew, clear focus states, strong contrast, reduced-motion support, accessible form labels, explicit WhatsApp contact consent, and non-duplicative screen-reader content for animated marquees.
