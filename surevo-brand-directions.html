Surevo — three brand directions

Three distinct visual identities for Surevo, the WhatsApp-first AI sales agent for Israeli WooCommerce stores. Each is built to stand apart from TukiAI's playful parrot world and to feel premium, local, and trustworthy. Pick one — or mix elements — and I'll build the full identity system from it.

v✓

One idea runs through all three: the “v” in Surevo is a checkmark — the “delivered” tick, the “sure thing,” the closed deal. It's the brand's signature gesture, recognisable even on its own.

Direction A

The Closer

Premium, decisive, intelligent. Indigo confidence with a “signal” mint accent. Reads like a serious B2B SaaS that closes deals — the trustworthy choice against a colourful competitor.
