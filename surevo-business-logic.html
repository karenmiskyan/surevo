07 · What this means for the website

The site has one job: book the audit

Because we sell done-for-you through a human-led funnel, the website is not a self-serve checkout. It's a trust-and-capture machine. Every design decision should serve this:

  • Primary CTA everywhere: “קבל אבחון מכירות חינם” (get a free sales audit) — one goal, not “sign up.”
  • Hebrew-first, RTL native — an Israeli must land on a page that feels built for them.
  • The live demo is the hero — Vero answering real Hebrew questions, closing a sale, front and centre.
  • Trust signals: real case study with ₪ recovered, Israeli payment/invoicing logos, the 30-day guarantee, “Made in Israel.”
  • Vero everywhere — the face that makes “hand your sales to AI” feel safe.
  • Price = reassurance, not a wall — show it's a managed service worth the setup, framed against money it recovers.
