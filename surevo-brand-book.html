The WhatsApp-native AI sales agent for Israeli WooCommerce stores. Meet Vero — the agent with a face your customers trust.

01 · Essence

What Surevo is

Surevo gives every online store a tireless seller that lives where Israeli customers already are — WhatsApp. Not a cold FAQ bot, but a warm, capable agent that recommends, reassures, and closes. The brand has to feel human, local, and certain. That's why we gave it a character, not just a logo.

02 · The mark

Meet Vero

Vero is a speech bubble with a face — the product itself, drawn. The bubble says “conversation.” The face makes it someone you trust. And the right eye is always a checkmark: the “v” in surevo, the WhatsApp double-tick, the closed deal. It's the one detail that must never change.

05 · Rules

Clear space, size & misuse

Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space equal to the height of the bubble's tail on all sides. Never place the logo smaller than: icon 24px, horizontal lockup 96px wide.

Never

  • ✕ Recolour outside the palette
  • ✕ Stretch, rotate, or skew the bubble
  • ✕ Swap the checkmark eye for a dot
  • ✕ Add shadows, gradients, or outlines
  • ✕ Place the colour logo on a busy photo (use reverse)
  • ✕ Re-typeset the wordmark in another font
